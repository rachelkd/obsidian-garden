> [!goal]- Learning Objectives
> - Obtain **probabilities**, **expected values**, **quantiles** for continuous probability distributions from pdfs and cdfs
> - Derive **cdfs** from pdfs and **pdfs** from cdfs for continuous probability distributions
> - Determine **moments** and identify continuous probability distributions with moment generating functions (mgfs)
> - Compute **expected values** and **variances** of functions of continuous random variables
> - Recognize phenomena that can be modelled by common continuous probability distributions
>     - such as **Uniform** and **Normal** distributions, and solve related problems
> - Simulate realizations of random variables using the $\text{Unif}(0,1)$ distribution (in tutorial)
